Arts & Humanities

A vibrant faculty covering Home Science, Physical Education, Music, and Fine Arts — nurturing creativity, culture, and well-being.

🏠 Home Science

B.A. Level

Training at B.A. level with practical work in a well-equipped laboratory. The department regularly organises Mehndi, Rangoli, Knitting, Cooking, and Embroidery competitions. An end-of-session exhibition-cum-sale of student handicrafts is a highlight of the academic year.

Students gain hands-on experience that bridges academic learning with practical life skills and creative expression.

BPES Program

Languages

Comprehensive language education in English, Hindi, Punjabi, and Sanskrit — shaping communication, literature, and cultural understanding.

🇬🇧 English

Elective + Compulsory

English is offered as both an elective and compulsory course at graduation level. The department shares a Language Lab with Hindi and Punjabi. An Add-on Course in Communicative English has been running since 2017. The Tagore Literary Society organises debates, quiz competitions, paper readings, and movie screenings to foster language skills.

🇮🇳 Hindi

Graduation Level

Hindi is offered at graduation level, improving communication skills and building student interest in classical and modern Hindi literature. The Hindi Sahitya Sabha organises extension lectures, declamation contests, paper reading sessions, and Hindi Diwas celebrations.

🌾 Punjabi

Post-Graduate

Punjabi is offered as compulsory and elective at graduation level, along with a Post Graduate course (M.A. Punjabi). The Punjabi Sahit Sabha organises debates, paper reading, and creative writing sessions to celebrate and promote Punjabi language and literature.

🕉️ Sanskrit

B.A. Elective

Sanskrit is offered as an elective subject at graduation level. Students have the unique opportunity to visit the Indological Center at VVRI Sadhu Ashram, Hoshiarpur — one of the premier Sanskrit research institutes in India — enriching their study of ancient Indian knowledge systems.

Social Sciences

📜 History

Post-Graduate B.A. Elective

History is offered as an elective at B.A. level. M.A. History was introduced in 2014–15, making it a full post-graduate discipline. The department draws on India's rich cultural heritage and prepares students for competitive examinations, civil services, and academic careers.
