✦ Alumni Spotlight

The Voice That Defined Villainy in Bollywood

Among DAV Hoshiarpur's most celebrated alumni, Late Sh. Amrish Puri rose to become one of the most iconic actors in Indian cinema. His powerful screen presence, commanding baritone, and unforgettable performances made him a legend that transcended generations.

His journey from the halls of DAV Hoshiarpur to the grandest stages of Bollywood is a testament to the institution's belief that great education cultivates great human beings — in every field of life.
