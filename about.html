About the College

Campus Profile

D.A.V. College Hoshiarpur

D.A.V. College Hoshiarpur is a premier multi-faculty Post Graduate institution of Northern India, with a legacy spanning over a century. Established in 1926, the college has continuously evolved to meet the demands of a changing world while staying rooted in the timeless values of the DAV tradition.

Accredited with NAAC Grade 'A' in January 2014 and affiliated to Panjab University, Chandigarh, the college has carved a distinguished niche in academics, sports, and extra-curricular activities.

The college strives to empower students through holistic education rooted in Indian cultural values, fostering intellectual growth, social responsibility, and global awareness — preparing them not just for careers but for life.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

🔭

Our Vision

To be a leading institution of higher education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, builds character, and produces graduates who contribute meaningfully to society — individuals who are academically excellent, culturally aware, and socially responsible.

We envision a campus where every student finds their potential, guided by the values of truth, righteousness, and service — the foundational pillars of the DAV movement.

🎯

Our Mission

  • Provide quality education irrespective of caste, creed, or socio-economic status.
  • Foster holistic development through academics, sports, arts, and community service.
  • Nurture intellectual growth rooted in Indian cultural values and ethical standards.
  • Prepare students for national and global challenges through relevant, skill-based education.
  • Maintain an inclusive, accessible, and inspiring campus environment for all students.
  • Build bridges between industry and academia through meaningful partnerships.
Facilities

Campus Infrastructure

A rich, well-maintained campus designed to support learning, creativity, and growth.

🎭

Hem Raj Kapoor Auditorium

State-of-the-art auditorium for cultural events, seminars, and institutional gatherings.

💻

Computer Labs

4 fully air-conditioned computer labs plus a multimedia room with high-speed internet connectivity.

📖

Library Complex

Well-stocked main library and a dedicated Post Graduate library with a rich collection of books and journals.

🏋️

Playgrounds & Gymnasium

Lush green playgrounds with a well-maintained athletics track and a fully-equipped gymnasium.

🔬

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Home Science, and Computer Science.

🎨

Fine Arts Studio

Dedicated studio space for fine arts students covering Still Life, Portrait, Landscape, and Clay work.

🌿

Botanical Garden

A serene botanical garden on campus fostering love for nature and environmental awareness.

🏛️

Heritage Room

Preserving the rich century-long history and cultural heritage of D.A.V. College Hoshiarpur.

🏦

Indian Bank Branch

A fully functional branch of Indian Bank located on campus for student and staff convenience.
