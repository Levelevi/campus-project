Who We Are

A Premier Institution of Northern India

D.A.V. College Hoshiarpur has been a beacon of quality education since 1926. Affiliated to Panjab University, Chandigarh and accredited with NAAC Grade 'A', the college has carved a distinguished niche in academics, sports, and extra-curricular activities.

The college strives to empower students through holistic education rooted in Indian cultural values, fostering intellectual growth, social responsibility, and global awareness.

🌟 Among our distinguished alumni is Late Sh. Amrish Puri — one of Bollywood's most legendary actors — a proud testament to the college's rich legacy.

🏆

Sports Excellence

Lush green playgrounds, athletics track, gymnasium and facilities for football, cricket, basketball, boxing, badminton, and more.

Placement Support

Active placement cell with industry partners including Infosys, IBM, Reliance JIO, Mahindra Finance, and Kotak Mahindra Life Insurance.

🎭

Cultural Activities

Annual talent hunts, youth festivals, debate clubs, fine arts, music, and literary societies that nurture all-round personality development.

🌱

NSS & NCC

Active NSS and NCC units conducting blood donation camps, cleanliness drives, tree plantation, and rural social service activities.
